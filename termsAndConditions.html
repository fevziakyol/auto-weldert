If an order is placed based on the cost estimate, any costs for the cost estimate shall be offset against the order invoice, and the total price may only be exceeded when calculating the order with the customer's consent.

§ 3 Completion

  1. The contractor is obliged to adhere to a completion date designated in writing as binding. If the scope of work changes or expands compared to the original order and this results in a delay, the contractor must immediately state a new completion date, indicating the reasons.

  1. The invoice shall separately show prices or price factors for each technically self-contained work performance, as well as for used spare parts and materials.   4. The value-added tax shall be borne by the customer.
  5. Any correction of the invoice by the contractor, as well as any complaint by the customer, must be made no later than 6 weeks after receipt of the invoice.

  1. The invoice amount and prices for ancillary services are due for payment in cash or by EC card upon acceptance of the repaired item and handover or dispatch of the invoice, but no later than 1 week after notification of completion and handover or dispatch of the invoice.
